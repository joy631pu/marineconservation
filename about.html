BIO

Hi, I am Joy, a marine conservationist who likes to take undersea experience and wants to raise marine awareness among people. I am an extrovert person and I really like to have fun with my friends and co-workers whenever I get enough time.

Goals + Values

"My vision is for healthy, protected and pollution free rivers and oceans and coastal environments and marine wildlife, which contributes to a sustainable and healthy planet in an equitable and rapidly changing world where community awareness, social upliftment and education is essential to enable people to understand, respect, value and participate in the wonder and diversity of nature and our oceans and all life on earth." (oceans-alive.org,2023)
